About Us

Welcome to our innovative project aimed at revolutionizing the way American Sign Language (ASL) is taught and learned. Our team has developed an interactive website that leverages cutting-edge technologies such as computer vision and deep learning, all built on the Flask framework, to provide a comprehensive and engaging learning experience for ASL enthusiasts.

At its core, our project combines the power of computer vision and deep learning algorithms to accurately interpret and recognize ASL gestures in real-time. By harnessing the capabilities of these advanced technologies, we aim to bridge the communication gap between the hearing and deaf communities by providing a user-friendly platform for learning and practicing ASL online.
