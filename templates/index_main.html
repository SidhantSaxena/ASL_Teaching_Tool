Want to Learn ASL ?

  • ASL is a complete, natural language used by the deaf and hard-of-hearing community.

  • ASL is a visual-gestural language that relies on hand movements, facial expressions, and body language to convey meaning and communicate thoughts, ideas, and emotions.

  • It plays a crucial role in facilitating interaction and inclusivity for individuals who are deaf or have hearing impairments.
